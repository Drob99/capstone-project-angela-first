My Projects

In this page, I will include some of the projects that I have worked on and what I achieved in each one.

ChartWise (KAUST Academy Project)

  1. Developed a multimodal RAG tool that takes text documents, images, and databases as input and retrieves them before generating domain-specific queries with a 90% accuracy.

  2. Awarded 3rd place among 50+ projects presented in the KAUST Academy's AI summer program final ceremony.

  3. Combined several technologies: Streamlit for frontend; PyTorch, LangChain, Chroma, and GPT for the AI model; Hugging Face and Kaggle for finding our data.

Saudi Railways System (Course Project)

  1. Developed a full-stack railway system using React.js, Express.js, and PostgreSQL, enabling passengers to book seats, process pay- ments, and manage loyaltyrewards efficiently.

  2. Designed and optimized database schema to handle complex railway operations, including train schedules, seat availability, waitlists, and staff assignments.

  3. Implemented key administrative functionalities for staff to manage reservations, assign train personnel, and generate reports on train occupancy, passenger details, and system efficiency.
